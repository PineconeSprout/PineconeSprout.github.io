The Rising of the Bear

Early developpement

Roguelike game that revolves around a zombie apocalypse, where a Build-A-Bear employee gets killed and reincarnated into a plushie from the store. After realizing his immortality the reincarnated main character goes out into the zombie apocalypse to find out the source of the breakout.

With you Again

Game Jam project

Co-op puzzle platformer, where you play as two spirits, that belonged to a broken pair, and this is your last chance at rekindling your love and relationship in the spirit world. Go through puzzles, help each other and start seeing the same colored spirit world as you advance in the game!

Download it on Itch.io!

Birdstrike

Game Jam project

2D clicker game where you play as a flight student that needs to survive a flight test. In order to pass the player must shoot the birds in the way of the plane to avoid a birdstrike, inform passengers of turbulence, and click the correct buttons in time.

Forestscape

Finished

Open world exploring game where you play as the son of a nature researcher, trying to find the same passion in nature as your deceased father. Get closure by exploring, taking pictures of wildlife, fix abandonned structures and fill out your fathers scrapbook.
